Chapter X: Encounters

Throughout their fight to survive the world of Mephitis, characters must face various encounters created by the GM. Encounters present difficult situations the characters must face and trials they must overcome. An encounter can take the form of a tense negotiation between a player character and an NPC, a daring feat of gymnastics across the rooftop of an apartment building, or a grisly fight against the infected.

Save Throws

A save throw is a special kind of skill check a survivor may perform in an attempt to mitigate negative effects. There are two types of save throws, Compelled and Improvised.

A Compelled save throw is a free save throw which a survivor is instructed to perform. Many rules and abilities instruct a survivor to make a save throw, such as certain attacks or when an encounter is initiated by the GM. The result of the save throw is detailed by the effect that instructed the survivor to perform it.
