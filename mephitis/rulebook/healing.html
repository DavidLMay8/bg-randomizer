Chapter X: Resting and Healing

Resting

The group should find time to rest often. Relaxation and recuperation allows characters to relieve stress, shake off overexertion, and heal damage. There are two forms of resting short and long, each with its own duration and benefits.

Short Rest

Often, the group won't have time for a full night's rest or a whole day of lounging around the safe house. A short rest offers a slight reprieve from the horrors of survival while allowing the group to get a quick move on.
