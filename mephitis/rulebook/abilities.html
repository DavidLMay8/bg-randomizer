Chapter X: Abilities

Abilities are special characteristics which make a character unique and help them survive the outbreak. During character creation, characters may purchase tiers in abilities based on their Attribute scores. Ability tiers are cumulative, meaning a character who has Tier 2 in an ability also has all the benefits of Tier 1, and a character who has Tier 3 in an ability has all the benefits of both Tiers 1 and 2.

Purchasing Abilities

While creating a character, players have a number of ability points they may spend for each of their attribute scores. A Body score of 3 gives the player three points which may be spent to purchase (Body) abilities. If an ability relates to multiple attributes, players may spend their ability points from any of those attributes and must record which attribute the ability is associated with. Each point spent on that ability must come from the same attribute. For example, a player purchasing Billy Goat (Body, Spirit) may spend either Body or Spirit points. If they purchase the ability using Body they record the ability on their character sheet as Billy Goat (Body), while if they purchase the ability using Spirit it is instead recorded as Billy Goat (Spirit).
