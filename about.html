Who Are We?

We are a small group of two individuals with a passion for board games. We discovered a need for a tool that would randomize board game components when we realized just how long and grueling game setup can be. We quickly jumped to work and developed the Board Game Configurator web app. The app was an instant success in the board game community, and soon we discovered there was a need for a more optimized version of the configurator in app form. We are pleased to announce the release of our BGC app on both the App Store and Google Play Store.
